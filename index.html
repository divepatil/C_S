Prep Techniques

Measure and grind fresh coffee beans: Use about 2 tablespoons of coffee per 6 ounces of water and grind your beans just before brewing for maximum freshness and flavor.

Prep Techniques

Use fresh, filtered water at the right temperature: Heat water to about 195-205°F (90-96°C), letting boiling water cool for 15-30 seconds before pouring, to avoid bitterness and extract the best taste

Prep Techniques

Brew with care using your preferred method: Whether French press, pour-over, or drip, saturate the grounds evenly, time the extraction (around 3-4 minutes for French press), then enjoy immediately for optimal flavor
